What is HandInHand?

HandInHand is an organ donation awareness and information platform that educates people about the importance of organ and tissue donation. The platform encourages individuals to register as donors and provides reliable information about the organ donation process.

How the Organ Donation Network Works

Organ donation operates within a regulated system that matches donated organs with patients who urgently need transplants. Medical professionals evaluate donors and allocate organs based on compatibility, urgency, waiting time, and location.

Why Organ Donation Matters

Organ donation saves lives. One donor can save up to eight lives through organ transplantation and help many more through tissue donation. It reduces waiting lists and gives patients a second chance at living full and healthy lives.
